Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Spendly ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described below.

2. Data We Collect

To create and maintain your account, we collect basic information such as your name and email address. To provide expense-tracking functionality, you voluntarily enter financial data — expense amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions — which is stored against your account. We also collect standard technical data (IP address, browser type, device information) needed to keep the service secure and operational.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate Spendly: to authenticate you, to store and display your expenses, to generate the category and time-period summaries you request, and to keep your account secure. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to providing the service.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers and is associated only with your account. Account credentials are hashed; we cannot read your password. You retain full ownership of the financial data you enter. You can export or delete your data at any time from your profile, and deletion is permanent — we do not retain copies after you request removal.

5. Third Party Services

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Limited service providers (such as hosting infrastructure) may process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality and data-protection obligations. These providers are not permitted to use your data for any purpose other than operating Spendly on our behalf.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

Spendly uses a small number of first-party cookies and browser storage to keep you signed in and to remember your preferences. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

7. Data Security

We apply industry-standard safeguards — encryption in transit, hashed credentials, access controls — to protect your information. No system is perfectly secure, but we work continuously to reduce risk and respond promptly to any incidents.

8. Your Rights

You may access, correct, export, or delete the personal data associated with your account at any time. If you have questions about exercising these rights, reach out through the contact channels below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the service evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Spendly after a change indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please reach out through the support channels provided within the application.
